Who I am

I live with Tomorrow 's picture

I’m am Hlulani Baloyi, 25 years old born in Limpopo. I work as a Digital Specialist at a consulting firm In my final year I joined Geekulcha, where I got to work with web and mobile application, in the process I developed my love for mobile applications, specifically android Geekulcha introduced me to attending more Tech events which in the process I got to realize that there is a serious gap between males and females in the industry. I realized that girls/women are sitting back which made me want to do something about this. So I started searching more of sites that speaks empowerment for women in the ICT sector, in the process I ran into Girl Rising site amongst other many sites, they encourage Girls education in general so I applied to be an ambassador for SA, which they appointed me. Girl Rising partners with Intel so when Intel found out about my appointment they got me to work closely with them with a focus on introducing girls to the ICT sector, where I get to run tutoring sessions as an intro to coding I also co-run meetups with girls who are already in the ICT sector (We call ourselves Raeketsetsa which means Doing it for ourselves) so we can build and share information as we build our portfolios in the industry, Under the Girl Rising umbrella, I also get to host career expos for high school students. Girl Rising Career Expo is an outreach program targeted at young people (High schools learners and the unemployed) from disadvantaged backgrounds found in South Africa’s townships. The program seeks to open opportunities for young people currently unemployed and those feeling uninspired in their educational pursuits. In reaching out to these youth, the outreach effort borrows from the African culture of storytelling while sharing information. The project envisages a central point where current industry leaders can share the stories of their journeys to success with the youth. Through this process it is hoped we can achieve a shift in mindset so these young people can realize their current circumstances are just a temporary situation they can change through choice. As history has clearly shown, teaching a person to fish without giving them a fishing rod aids them in no way. The Girl Rising Career Expos are usually held in Atteridgeville, Pretoria I got to be featured in a number of articles including Htxt.women, Tech Girl, Sunday Times, Intel IQ site, Digify Africa and Rekord amongst others. My major achievement was to score a job at IBM after being headhunted.
